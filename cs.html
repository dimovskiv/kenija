CLASSES

Classes run on a term basis. CHOICE INTEGRATED ACADEMY classes run on a term (3 months) basis. Terms begin in January, May and September, respectively. During school holidays in August and December every year, we hold a special one week camp to any students who want to join.

Educational trip Schedule is available when you contact the Choice Integrated Academy school administration via any of the following email address: Info@choiceintegratedacademy.co.ke
