Choice integrated Academy Learning Trajectory

At Choice integrated Academy; for computer science, each course is comprised of three terms (1 year). Also, each stage is comprised of three courses (3 years). After completing one stage, a student advances to the next stage. This learning path enables students to learn each course in-depth and become true problem-solvers in computer science. The Science with Experiments course content is based on the UK’s science curriculum. Students will learn the principles of science that occur in their daily lives by doing the experiments hands-on. This science course enables students to visually and logically understand science for effective learning.

What Makes Choice Integrated Academy Special?

MULTI-DIMENSIONAL STEM EDUCATION

With research-based teaching and powerful technology, we bring out the best in your child, elevate, and equip them with essential skills required in the 21st century. Our STEM education structure includes unplugged, hands-on activities, programming/coding (MIT Scratch, Python) as well as physical computing (LittleBits: easy-to-use electronic building blocks , BBC Micro:bit, Arduino, etc.) to encourage applied computational thinking through creativity.

SMALL CLASSROOM SIZES

A majority of our classes have an impressively low student to teacher ratio. Small class sizes and an inspiring and highly qualified teaching faculty ensure that students get the absolute most out of their education. Experience the benefits of a small class size and you will know why we believe that it’s an integral part of a world-class education.

Authorized persons (to pick up your child/children)

You are required to provide details of the person/s authorized to collect your child on the application form. Kindly reconfirm these details with the teacher/minder to who you hand over your child. If you are sending someone other than the usual authorized person to pick up your child, you must either inform the duty teacher in the morning or send the “collector” with an authorization note signed by the parent/guardian.

Morning and afternoon car procedures

We urge all parents, drivers, nannies to be extremely cautious when dropping off their children. Ensure you keep hold of your child in the parking area and drive very carefully. Students can remain in the car and be collected from the car by the teacher/minder on duty. 5km/hr recommended speed within the school compound.

Accidents

Whilst every care is taken of the children, accidents do happen under unavoidable circumstances. In the event of minor injuries first aid will be administered at the school. In the event a more serious incident, the child will be taken to the nearest and medical facility:

Child’s Progress

Parent – teacher meetings are held at the end of every term to discuss your child’s progress. Parents who wish to check on their child’s progress should make an appointment with the class teacher in advance, through the office and a convenient time will be arranged. ALL visitors (including parents) must pass through the office and NOT go directly to the classrooms. Please follow these directions as they are put in place for the security of the children.

School-Home Communication

At Choice Integrated Academy, school-home communication and vice-versa is very important to us. We will communicate with you on a regular basis through newsletters detailing important activities and events. These will be sent with your child or on email. Each student has a diary which can be used to communicate with the class teacher or school.

Contact Details

The school office must have your updated current address and telephone numbers for your child. We all require additional contacts if we are unable to reach parents in case of an emergency. Please notify the school of your new address in writing.

PREPARTORY OVERVIEW (6-11 Yrs)

Curriculum

Prep School offers the British National Curriculum and caters for pupils aged 6-11 years. The Prep School Section consists of Key Stage I for ages 5 — 7 Years and Key Stage 2 for ages 7 – 11 Years. Curriculum delivery is child- Centered. Skill- based and focuses on the holistic development of the individual child.

Core Subjects

Literacy. Numeracy and Science Foundation Subjects These are History, Music, Geography. Art and Design. Physical Education. Swimming. ICT. Swahili. French. German ,Islamic and Christian Religious Education, Fiq, Arabic,Quaran.Aqaid, Tarikh .

Co-curricular Activities

These include Poetry Competitions, Drama, Science Fair, Mathematics, and Science Challenge. Humanities Week and Musicals.

Extra-Curricular Activities

These include Sports, Club Activities, School Choir, Drama, Trips, Ballet, LAMDA. Band Ensemble and Community Service,
